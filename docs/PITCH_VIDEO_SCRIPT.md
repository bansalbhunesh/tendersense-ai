# TenderSense AI — pitch / demo video script

**Target length:** 75–90 seconds (submission-friendly). **Tone:** confident, plain language, one breath per line where marked.

**Before you record:** Open the app logged in, have a tender with criteria + 2 bidders ready, `demo/pdfs/` nearby. Record **1080p**, face + screen or voiceover + screen.

---

## Cold open — hook (0:00–0:08)

*[Face or bold on-screen text. No logo yet.]*

> **“One wrong eligibility call on a government tender doesn’t just lose a bid — it loses trust.”**  
> *[Pause half a beat.]*  
> **“TenderSense AI makes sure that never happens in the dark.”**

*[Cut to product logo + title card: TenderSense AI — 5 seconds max.]*

---

## Why — the pain (0:08–0:22)

> **“Indian tenders are huge PDFs, mixed Hindi and English, and bidder evidence that doesn’t line up.”**  
> **“Officers drown in documents — and brittle keyword tools either miss nuance or hide mistakes.”**  
> **“We built for three non‑negotiables:”**  
> *[On-screen bullets as you say each:]*  
> **“Never silently reject.”**  
> **“Every verdict traceable.”**  
> **“Every override auditable.”**

---

## What — the product in one sentence (0:22–0:32)

> **“TenderSense is an explainable evaluation stack: ingest tender + bidder PDFs, extract criteria, run a decision engine, and route uncertainty to humans — not to a black box.”**

*[Quick montage: dashboard → tender workspace → one PDF upload if you have time.]*

---

## How — the demo spine (0:32–1:05)

**Beat 1 — Ingest (10s)**  
> **“Upload the tender PDF — OCR and extraction pull structured criteria you can see and edit.”**

**Beat 2 — Bidders (10s)**  
> **“Register bidders, attach evidence — the system normalises what it reads and flags what’s missing.”**

**Beat 3 — Run (10s)**  
> **“Hit evaluate — jobs run in the background; you’re not staring at a spinner for ten minutes.”**

**Beat 4 — The money shot (15s)**  
> **“Here’s the reasoning graph: every ELIGIBLE, NOT_ELIGIBLE, and NEEDS_REVIEW ties back to a clause and a snippet.”**  
> *[Zoom a node; tap NEEDS_REVIEW if you have one.]*  
> **“NEEDS_REVIEW means: we’re not guessing — we’re handing the officer the decision.”**

**Beat 5 — Trust layer (8s)**  
> **“Review queue, overrides, hash‑chained audit log — built for procurement, not for a hackathon slide.”**

## Engagement tricks (use 2–3, don’t stack all)

| Hook | When |
|------|------|
| **Pattern interrupt** | Open with a question: *“When was the last time you trusted a black‑box ‘AI said no’ on a crore‑plus tender?”* |
| **Concrete number** | *“Eleven criteria, two bidders, one graph — watch the evidence chain.”* |
| **Micro‑tension** | Before Evaluate: *“Watch what happens when the engine is honest about ambiguity.”* |
| **Payoff line** | After graph: *“That’s not a score. That’s a receipt.”* |
| **Sovereignty nod** | One line: *“Sovereign mode: deterministic path when you need zero foreign cloud.”* |

## B‑roll checklist (silent shots you can drop under voiceover)

- Dashboard with tender cards  
- PDF dropping into upload zone  
- Criteria list with ≥1 row expanded  
- Evaluate button → job status chip **completed**  
- Reasoning graph with colours + side panel  
- Review queue two‑pane (even empty state with copy)  
- Audit log or SECURITY one‑liner on screen  

---

## One‑page “legendary” cheat sheet (memorise this order)

1. **Hook** — trust / silent rejection  
2. **Why** — PDF chaos + accountability gap  
3. **What** — one sentence product  
4. **How** — 4 demo beats + graph payoff  
5. **Trust** — audit + officer in the loop  
6. **Proof** — prod auth / repo (optional)  
7. **Close** — CTA + URL  

---

## Recording notes

- **Pace:** Slightly slower than conversation on dense lines; punch the hooks.  
- **Audio:** Lav or headset; normalize loudness; no music under critical lines (or keep it −18 LUFS under voice).  
- **Failsafe:** If live demo flakes, keep a **screen recording** from a green run and voiceover live — judges forgive stability, not confusion.
